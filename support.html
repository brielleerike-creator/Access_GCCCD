Accessibility Requests

Request Accessibility Support

We’re here to help. Submit a request below or explore additional accessibility resources.

How can we support you?

Thank you for contacting the Access GCCCD team. Whether you have a question about course accessibility, websites, documents, instructional technology, or digital accessibility compliance, we are here to help.

We strive to respond within 48–72 business hours, Monday through Friday.

Submit a Request

Accessibility Support Form

Before submitting your request

Please do not include confidential, sensitive, or personally identifiable information, such as Social Security numbers, medical records, or passwords. If additional information is needed, a member of the Access GCCCD team will contact you directly.
